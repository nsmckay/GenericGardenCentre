FOODHALL

Located on the ground floor of our main building, our vast foodhall should provide you with a wide range of foods, drinks and spices. Try our artisan baking and confectionery, and wash it down with some of our flavoursome drinks! If you're looking for something more exotic, we also have a delicatetessen butcher. And don't forget to visit us at Christmas time, because you won't want to miss our festive hampers and gift boxes!
