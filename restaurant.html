RESTAURANT

Located in the conservatory, our luxurious restaurant should take care of all of your dining needs. Whether you're hungry for a breakfast, desperate for lunch, or simply want a snack between meals, we have a wide range of options to satisfy your hunger at an affordable cost. Check out our menu below to see what we have in store for you! Our dining area is very large, with an upstairs level with extra tables, so you won't need to worry about not finding a seat. If you'd like to enjoy your food in the ambience of nature, there are even tables for you to sit outside, not to mention a playing area for the kids situated far away enough for you to not be bothered by their noise!
