PETS

If you look after any animals, you'll want to take a look at our pets section. We have cages, beds, brushes, toys to keep your pet fit and active, and nutritious foods to keep them healthy. The staff here are all animal lovers, and will be happy to give you tips on how to best look after your pets. If you don't look after any pets, but are interested in doing so, you can adopt one of the ones we have taken care of. Just be sure to take good care of it! We have an aviary with many bright, colourful parakeets to choose from, and an aquarium filled with exciting tropical fish. If you like animals but don't feel like you can manage looking after one, why not try some of our bird seed and help look after your feathered garden visitors?
