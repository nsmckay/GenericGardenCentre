PLANTS

Step out into our garden on a nice day, and admire our beautiful collection of plantlife. All of the lovely flowers, bushes and trees you see are well looked after by our dedicated staff, and if you are a budding gardener yourself, they'll be all too happy to give you some advice on how to look after your own. Check out our greenhouse to see our most exotic plants, including tropical flowers from the rainforests of South America. And if you aren't much of a gardener, why not try some of our indoor cacti and succulents. They're easy to look after, and just as pretty!
